You work for a company that develops logistics software for 
construction companies. You want to understand how economic 
outlook affects spending on infrastructure project. You 
start by looking at total construction spending in the US.

You have two variables from the US Bureau of Transportation
Statistics that have data from Jan-2005 to Jan-2023, with one
data point per month. The variables are defined as follows:

labor_force:   US labor force particiption (percent)
construction:  total dollar value of construction done in the US

Each variable has been added to your workspace.

Note: Labor force participation is the percentage of 
civilians who are working or are looking for work.
